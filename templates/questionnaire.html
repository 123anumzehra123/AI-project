Mental Health Screening

1. Do you experience extreme emotional shifts?

2. Do you feel a strong need to repeat actions (e.g. checking, cleaning)?

3. Do you find it difficult to stay focused for long periods?

4. Have you ever experienced flashbacks of traumatic events?

5. Do you worry excessively about being left or abandoned?

6. Do you feel like there are “different parts” of you?

7. Do you act impulsively or have difficulty with self-control?

8. Do you avoid people, places, or events that remind you of past trauma?

9. Do you regularly feel nervous or anxious without a clear cause?

10. Do you feel like you’re observing yourself from the outside?
