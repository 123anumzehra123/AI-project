Mental Health Tips & Guidance

Practical advice for managing different mental health conditions

ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder)

A neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity.

Management Tips:

  • Break tasks into smaller, manageable steps
  • Use timers and alarms to stay on track
  • Create consistent daily routines
  • Minimize distractions in your workspace

OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorder)

A disorder characterized by unwanted recurring thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions).

Management Tips:

  • Practice delaying compulsive responses
  • Challenge obsessive thoughts with logic
  • Gradually expose yourself to triggers
  • Establish a worry time limitation

BPD (Borderline Personality Disorder)

A mental health disorder characterized by emotional instability, distorted self-image, and intense relationships.

Management Tips:

  • Practice mindfulness and grounding techniques
  • Identify and label your emotions
  • Develop healthy coping mechanisms
  • Maintain a consistent daily schedule

PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder)

A disorder that develops after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event.

Management Tips:

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Gradually confront trauma memories safely
  • Maintain connections with supportive people
  • Establish a safe and comfortable environment
